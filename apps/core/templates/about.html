About

Welcome to Bennie+!

Our Story

Bennie+ was born from a shared love for extraordinary flavors and the desire to share those experiences with the world. Our founder, Ben, embarked on a global gastronomic adventure, discovering hidden culinary gems and connecting with artisans who pour their heart and soul into creating exceptional food products. Inspired by these discoveries, Bennie+ was established to bridge the gap between discerning food enthusiasts and the finest flavors the world has to offer.

What We Offer

At Bennie+, we take pride in offering a diverse range of handpicked food products that span continents and cultures. From velvety Swiss chocolates to aromatic spices from the bustling markets of India, from single-origin coffee beans sourced directly from the highlands of Ethiopia to organic teas harvested from serene Japanese gardens – our selection is a testament to our dedication to quality.

But we're more than just a culinary haven. We understand that life is a mosaic of interests and desires, which is why we've expanded our offerings to include a carefully curated collection of lifestyle products. Explore exquisite kitchenware that elevates your cooking experience, discover artisanal home decor that adds a touch of elegance to your living spaces, and find thoughtful gifts that convey your appreciation for loved ones.

Our Philosophy

Quality is our cornerstone. We believe that every product in our catalog tells a story – the story of the hands that crafted it, the traditions it embodies, and the joy it brings to those who savor it. Our team of experts rigorously evaluates each item to ensure it meets our stringent standards of taste, authenticity, and sustainability. We're committed to supporting local communities, promoting fair trade practices, and minimizing our environmental footprint.

Experience Bennie+

We invite you to explore our digital aisles with the same excitement as wandering through a bustling marketplace. Immerse yourself in the aromas, flavors, and textures that define cultures across the globe. Whether you're a passionate home cook, a food aficionado seeking new horizons, or someone looking for unique gifts that leave a lasting impression, Bennie+ has something special for you.

Thank you for joining us on this delectable journey. Your satisfaction is our utmost priority, and we're here to assist you in every step of your shopping experience. Welcome to Bennie+ – where every bite, every sip, and every discovery is an invitation to savor life's finer moments.

Savor the world with Bennie+ today!
